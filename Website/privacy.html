Rudolstadt-Festival (unofficial)

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Festival data

The app downloads festival data from the official website of the "Rudolstadt Festival", including program information, artist details, locations, maps, and news.

Your festival profile and iCloud sync

Data generated in the app, such as saved events, artist ratings, badge settings, and artist notes, is stored on your device. On iOS, the app can use Apple's iCloud service to keep this data up to date across your own devices. This sync uses CloudKit in the app's iCloud container. The data is not sent to a separate server operated by the developer and is not used for advertising or tracking.

Sharing with friends

If you choose to share your festival profile, invited friends can see your shared profile badge, saved events, and artist ratings through iCloud sharing. Artist notes are not included in shared profiles; they remain private to you and only sync across your own devices.

Analytics and tracking

The app does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.
